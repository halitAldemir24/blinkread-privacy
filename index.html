1. Information we collect

To provide the app's features, we collect the following:

Account information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address, display name, and a unique user identifier provided by the sign-in provider. Sign in with Apple may provide a relay email (@privaterelay.appleid.com) instead of your real address if you choose to hide it.

Reading data

  • Books you upload or add to your library (stored in your private cloud storage)
  • Reading progress, last-read position, and chapter state
  • Reading preferences (theme, text size, WPM, fonts, RSVP settings)
  • Onboarding answers and reading goals

Subscription data

Purchase receipts and subscription status are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) and relayed to our subscription provider, RevenueCat, to unlock premium features. We do not see or store your payment method, card number, or billing address.

Technical data

Standard platform-level information such as device model, OS version, and anonymous installation identifiers may be processed by our service providers (Firebase, Google, Apple) to keep the app running. BlinkRead does not include any third-party advertising or marketing SDKs.

2. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To sync your library, reading progress, and preferences across devices
  • To verify your subscription status and unlock premium features
  • To diagnose crashes and technical problems
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the app.

4. Subscriptions & purchases

BlinkRead offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly, annual) and a lifetime purchase. Monthly and annual subscriptions may include a 3-day free trial for eligible new customers. Payments are processed exclusively by Apple or Google; we never receive your payment credentials.

Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours beforehand through your device's store settings. You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

5. Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your profile, library metadata, reading progress, and uploaded files from our active systems within a reasonable period. Backups may retain copies for a short additional window before being overwritten in the normal course of business.

6. Your rights & account deletion

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and stop using the app at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
Delete your account directly in the app: open Settings → Account → Delete Account. This removes your account and associated data from our servers. You can also email us at the address below and we will process the request manually.

7. Children's privacy

BlinkRead is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We rely on industry-standard security practices from our infrastructure providers, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit, Firebase security rules to restrict access to your data, and platform-level protections provided by iOS and Android. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using commercially reasonable safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you inside the app. Continued use of the app after the changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
